About Us

A technology driven early childhood education centre for children aged two to five years of age. Our goal is to understand your child’s strengths and needs, and work to build on their strengths. Our higher goal is to meet the needs of your child with an ultimate goal of promoting the holistic development of your child. Our staff is trained to make learning fun!

Your child’s classroom teacher and assistant teacher are trained to help your child develop and build on skills through developmentally appropriate activities. Your child will participate in a variety of experiential and play activities that will promote cognitive (thinking), language, pre-literacy, gross/fine motor skills and social skills.

We are all here to support you the parents. Having a young child or children is an exciting time in life, but can also be stressful and exhausting. We will do all that we can to learn about your child’s individual needs and point you in the right direction for more help about your child’s specific needs if necessary.

We want to help your child develop a love of learning that will be the beginning of becoming a life-long learner. Enjoy each stage your child is at, as they grow up so quickly. Thank you for choosing A 2 Zed Learning Centre as the roots to your child’s education.

A 2 Zed Learning Centre is committed to the provision of quality innovative learning solutions, that can be measured in terms of education concept, practice, curricula quality, interactions with teachers and students and meeting the needs of families and local communities

Our Team

Our teachers and assistant teachers have credentials and or training in Early Childhood and Primary School Education. . All members of our staff are dedicated to and believe in the quality of our school. Our staff goal is to provide a safe, nurturing and stimulating environment for all of the preschool children while recognizing children for their individuality.
