Our Classes

Pre
Pre-K class consists of children between the ages of two and three. Colouful, Stumlating Classroom designed for these toddlers. A caring staff will oversee the devlopment of these special children.
K1
K1 class consists of our four year olds. Students focus on developing fine and gross motor skills and self-help skills through various play based activities to help them become more aware of themselves and their peers.
K2
K2 class consists of our five year olds. This group masters the alphabet, sight and phonetic sound recognition, number recognition1-100, formal letter formation in writing our letters and sequential direction following.
